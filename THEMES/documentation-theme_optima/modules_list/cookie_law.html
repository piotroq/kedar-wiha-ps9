Configure Pos Cookie Law

  1. Login your Prestashop admin

  2. Go to POSTHEMES > Modules > Cookie law

  3. In SETTINGS:

    We got:

    • Cookie law text : Put here your own cookie law text
    • Background color : Color of background of cookie law
    • Text color : Color of text

    => Configure parameter as you want

  4. Click Save button when you finish

Translate text of module

  • Go to POSTHEMES > Modules > Cookie law

  • Click to choose language you want to translate.
  • At Cookie law text : Translate.
  • Click Save button when you finish
