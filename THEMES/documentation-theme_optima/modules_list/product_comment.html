Configure Pos Product Comments

  1. Login your Prestashop admin

  2. Go to Modules > Modules Manager

  3. Choose “Modules” tab

  4. On Pos Product Comments, click "Configure" button.

    We got:

    * In Configuration:

    • All reviews must be validated by an employee : Define whether all reviews must be validated by an employee or not
    • Allow guest reviews : Define whether guest reviews will be allowed or not
    • Minimum time between 2 reviews from the same user

    * In Reviews waiting for approval: All reviews are waiting for approval. Click Approve at each review to show.

    * In Reported Reviews

    * In Review Criterions : All review criterions. Click Edit to edit.

    * In Approved Reviews : All approved reviews.

    => Configure parameter as you want

  5. Click Save button when you finish
